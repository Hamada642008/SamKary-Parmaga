Operators part1

Operators: are used to perform operations on variables and values

Examples:

  • Arithmetic
  • Assignment
  • Comparison
  • Logical
  • Bitwise
  • Membership
  • Identity

1. Arithmetic operators: are used with numeric values to perform common mathematical operations like: [+, -, /, *, %, **, //].

Examples:

  • print(10+2) #Addition
  • print(3*2) #Multipication
  • print(3**2) #Exponational
  • print(8/2) #Division
  • print(8//2) #Floor divition
  • print(9%2) #Modulus

Output:

  • 12
  • 6
  • 8
  • 4.0
  • 4
  • 1

2. Assignment: are used to assign values to variables like: [=, +=, -=, *=, /=, %=, **=, //=].

Examples:

  • x= 4
  • y= 5
  • x=y
  • print(x)

Output:

  • 5
